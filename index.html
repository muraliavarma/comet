Carved along the canvas of a billion stars
Gasps an icy stone for its final breath
Engulfed in flames, putting on a sublime show
Ephemeral and magnificent in death

Through a billion such swan songs, I drift
Inevitably like a moth toward a flame
My latest lap just beginning
Gently growing, glowing bright
In my wake, seeding life

And then I burn layer upon layer
Spill the secrets from far away places
Before embarking on a familiar trail
Fading away into the dying light

To one day return a different person
With stories to tell you, my future children
Of hidden truths from the deep unknown
Of your ancestors who live forever
Inside both you and me
